Assessment Philosophy

Why we use confidence-based self-assessment

Why Confidence-Based?

We use confidence self-assessment rather than knowledge quizzes because it better supports adult learning

  • 1

    Adult Learning

    Professionals know when they've internalized something. Self-assessment respects that knowledge and encourages honest reflection.

  • 2

    Reduces Anxiety

    No "test" pressure that detracts from learning. The goal is growth, not gatekeeping.

  • 3

    Action-Oriented

    Focuses on "can you do this?" not "do you remember this?" — practical skills matter more than memorization.

  • 4

    Self-Directed

    Empowers learners to identify their own gaps and take ownership of their development.

How Assessments Work

Each path ends with a structured self-assessment

Assessment Components

  • Self-rating on key competencies (1-5 scale)
  • Suggested next steps based on ratings
  • Practical exercises to confirm readiness

Certificates Are Earned By:

  • Completing all modules
  • Achieving average confidence of 4+ across all competencies
  • Completing hands-on demonstration (Intermediate+)
