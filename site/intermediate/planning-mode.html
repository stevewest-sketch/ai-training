Planning Mode — The Motion Critical

Adopt the outcome-creator mindset and collaborate through planning

"There's a mental reframe when we're working with more powerful AI agents. They turn from task-doers to outcome creators."
— Austin, AI Lab Session

Learning Objective

By the end of this module, you'll adopt the outcome-creator mindset and learn how to collaborate with Claude through planning rather than task-by-task instruction.

The Mental Reframe

Stop thinking of Claude as something you give instructions to. Start thinking of it as a collaborator you share context with. This is the single most important shift in how you work with AI.

Voice Dump

Step 1 of 6 • 15-20 minutes

Record your thoughts about your idea using Apple Voice Memo or any recording app. Don't worry about structure—just talk through what you want to build, why, and any considerations.

Pro tip: Walk around while recording. Movement helps ideas flow. Don't edit yourself—capture everything, even half-formed thoughts.

The Interview Prompt

Use this exact prompt to have Claude interview you about your project

planning-prompt.md
Read this @SPEC.md file and interview me in detail using the AskUserQuestionTool about literally anything: technical implementation, UI & UX, concerns, tradeoffs, etc. but make sure the questions are not obvious. Be very in-depth and continue interviewing me continually until it's complete, then write the detailed spec to the file.
Key Insight

The interview process is where the magic happens. Claude will ask you questions you hadn't considered, surface edge cases, and help you think through tradeoffs. This is why planning mode reduces iteration—you're solving problems before they exist.

Review Before Approving

Read the plan thoroughly before approving. If technical details are unclear, ask Claude to explain them in non-technical terms. It's much easier to change a plan than to rewrite implemented code.

Self-Assessment

Before moving on, can you:

  • Create a SPEC.md from a voice dump or written description?
  • Run the interview prompt to refine requirements?
  • Review a plan and identify areas needing clarification?
