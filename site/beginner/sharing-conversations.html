Sharing Conversations

Collaborate with teammates on AI-assisted work

Learning Objective

By the end of this module, you'll know how to share conversations with colleagues and understand when different sharing options are appropriate.

Why Share?

  • Collaborate with teammates on AI-assisted work
  • Hand off context when you're OOO
  • Save valuable exchanges for future reference

How to Share

  1. Open the conversation you want to share
  2. Click the Share button (top right)
  3. Choose sharing option: Link sharing (anyone with link can view) or Team sharing (specific Gladly users)

Sharing options: project collaborators, chat link sharing, and privacy defaults.

What Gets Shared?

  • Full conversation history
  • All artifacts created
  • Any attached files

What Doesn't Get Shared?

  • Your connected services (Drive, Slack access)
  • Personal settings or preferences
Tip: Sharing vs. Projects

Sharing is great for one-off collaborations. For ongoing team work with persistent context, consider using Projects (covered in the next module).
